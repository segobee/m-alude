PROJECT 2

Faulty Water Pump Prediction

This project seeks to build a smart product (model) that predicts in real-time a point where a waterpump would fail due to some inherent faulty issues. It gives the operator heads up to make timely repairs on the water pumps to avoid supply disruption of clean and portable water in Tanzania Rural Communities

The How:

  • Sourced data from Drivendata platform
  • Trained an XgBoost model with 91% accuracy
  • Hypertuned the model's parameters to improve the model performance
  • Deployed the model with AWS Elastic Beanstalk

PROJECT 4

Customer Churn Analytics

For a business to survive in a saturated market, they must be at the front to predict WHO, WHEN, WHERE, AND HOW a customer would churn their product for other competitor product. In this project, I analysed historical data of of POWERCO COMPANY in relation to customers churning the business. The insights from the analysis aided the building of a classifier model to predict the customers that are likely to churn, so, the marketing dept of the company can target them to retain them.

The How:

  • Analyzed the data set, which gave rise to insightful outcome on influencing factors on customer churning
  • Trained a Random Forest Classifier model
  • Engineered some features that could have more predictive power on the model performance
  • Hypertuned the model's parameters to improve the model performance
  • Prescribed potential solutions to mitigate churn.
